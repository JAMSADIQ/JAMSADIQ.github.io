I am a postdoctoral research candidate at SISSA at Trieste, Italy since February 2023. I was postdoctoral researcher at IGFAE at USC Santiago de Compostela, Spain from November 2020 till February 2023. I have complted my PhD from Rochester Institute of Technology. My research focus on Gravitational Wave data analysis. Specifically I worked on rates and population studies of binary black holes in the universe. I also work on PyGRB search which a modelled search for gravitational waves involving concident gamma ray detections. I developed a code on hybridizing numerical relativity and post Newtonian waveforms for precessing binary black holes. In my PhD, I also learn skills in numerical relativity simulation of binary black holes. In my research project, I developed a method to test accuracy of an anlytical spacetime for binary black holes by comparing analytical evolution of this spacetimes it with numerical relativity evolution.
